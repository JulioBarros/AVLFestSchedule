Welcome to the unofficial AVLFest schedule.

I got tired of the festival only providing a non searchable image of the schedule so I created this using data from Reddit user @EatAtDaves Post

And actually I created it use Aider and claude-3-5-sonnet-20240620 in a few minutes ... then I got bored and went back to work. It's not perfect but I hope you find it useful.

Use the filters on the left to customize your view and find the shows you're most interested in.

We're crowd sourcing the extra data. Thanks to Ryan Appleton of Lazy Tiger Hostel for kicking it off.

All data subject to error and change so check with the venue or official schedule before making plans.
